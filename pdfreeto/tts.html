Free Text to Speech Online

PDFreeto's Text to Speech tool converts any written text into spoken audio directly in your browser, using your device's built-in voices. Choose from dozens of languages and voices, adjust the reading speed and pitch, and listen instantly — no account or installation needed.

How to Use Text to Speech

Paste or type your text, select a voice and language, then click Speak. The current word is highlighted as it's read aloud. You can pause and resume at any time. The audio download feature records the speech and saves it as a WAV file (available in Chrome and Edge).
