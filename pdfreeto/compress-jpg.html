How to Compress JPG Images for Free

PDFreeto compresses your JPG, PNG, and WEBP images directly in your browser using the HTML Canvas API. Adjust the quality slider to balance file size and visual quality, optionally resize the image, and download your compressed files instantly — no signup, no limits.

Why Compress Images?

Smaller images load faster on websites, take up less storage, and are easier to send by email. Compressing a JPG to 75% quality typically reduces file size by 50–70% with barely any visible difference — ideal for web publishing, social media, and sharing.
