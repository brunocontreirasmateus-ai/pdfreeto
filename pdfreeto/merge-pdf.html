How to Merge PDF Files for Free

PDFreeto combines multiple PDF files into a single document entirely in your browser. Upload your PDFs, drag to arrange them in the exact order you want, then click Merge. The resulting PDF is ready to download immediately — no data is ever sent to a server.

Common Uses for Merging PDFs

Merging PDFs is useful for combining contracts with appendices, assembling multi-chapter reports, joining scanned documents, or bundling invoices and receipts. PDFreeto preserves the original page layout and quality of each document in the merged output.
